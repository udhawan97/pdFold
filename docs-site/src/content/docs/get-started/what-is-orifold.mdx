Orifold is a PDF **workspace**, not a single-file editor. A "simple PDF task" is rarely one file — it's six PDFs, two screenshots, a Word document, and a scanned form. Orifold's job is to take all of that in, let you organize and finish it in one place, and hand back one polished file.

Everything happens on your Mac. There's no upload step, no account, and no server in the loop — import, editing, OCR, compression, encryption, and export all run locally.

<Figure src="/Orifold/assets/orifold-workspace-diagram.svg" alt="Orifold product flow: import mixed files, edit and finish them in one local workspace, then export one clean file — everything runs on your Mac" />

## The three-step shape of every task

1. **Import** — drop in PDFs, Word docs, images, scans, Markdown, HTML, CSV, even corrupt files.
2. **Work the workspace** — reorder and rotate pages, edit text, annotate, fill forms, sign, stamp, OCR.
3. **Export** — one clean PDF (or DOCX, Markdown, HTML, images) leaves your Mac exactly once, when you say so.

## Who it's for

Anyone doing PDF paperwork on a Mac who doesn't want a subscription, an upload, or a small ceremony to get a document finished — students, freelancers, small businesses, and anyone tired of `final_final_revised_ACTUAL.pdf`.
