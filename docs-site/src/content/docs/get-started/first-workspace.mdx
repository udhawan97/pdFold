## Steps

1. **Launch Orifold.** Double-click `Orifold.command` on your Desktop, or open the app from `~/Applications`.
2. **Pick a companion.** On first launch you'll choose **Gami** (the origami Bernedoodle) or **Ori** (the origami Siberian) — you can switch anytime.

   <Figure src="/Orifold/assets/screenshots/first-workspace-empty-state.png" alt="The empty-state screen just after picking a companion, with Gami the origami Bernedoodle in the corner and a short greeting bubble, above the drop zone for adding files." caption="The empty-state screen, right after picking a companion." />

3. **Drop in a few files.** Drag 2–3 PDFs, images, or a Word document onto the empty-state screen.
4. **Arrange them.** In the sidebar, drag pages into the order you want across all the files you dropped in.
5. **Export once.** Press <Kbd>⌘E</Kbd> or use **File → Export**, choose PDF, and save.

<Callout variant="tip">
	Merging isn't a separate step — the workspace *is* the merge. Arrange pages in the sidebar until they tell the story you need, then export once.
</Callout>

## What you should see

One exported PDF containing every file you dropped in, in the order you arranged them, saved wherever you chose in the save panel.

You now know most of what Orifold does day to day.
