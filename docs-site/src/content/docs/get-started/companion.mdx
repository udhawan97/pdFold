Orifold ships with a small built-in companion who reacts to what you do with short tips and the occasional wisecrack — helpful when you're new, easy to silence when you're not.

<Figure src="/Orifold/assets/screenshots/companion-gami-ori.svg" alt="Gami the origami dog and Ori the origami cat shown side by side in the corner of a workspace." caption="Gami (left) and Ori (right), the Orifold companions." />

## Choosing a companion

Pick **Gami** (the origami dog) or **Ori** (the origami cat) on first launch. Gami is loyal and endlessly encouraging; Ori is clever, composed, and quietly in charge. Switch anytime from the app menu.

## What they react to

Highlighting, signing, exporting, and warnings — Gami's tail wags faster the closer your cursor gets; Ori's ears twitch and tail sways.

## Turning them off

Use **Show Orifold Buddy** in the app menu to hide the companion. Everything else in Orifold works exactly the same either way.

<Callout variant="note">
	Charming when you want it, invisible when you don't — the toggle is a one-click, no-questions-asked switch.
</Callout>
