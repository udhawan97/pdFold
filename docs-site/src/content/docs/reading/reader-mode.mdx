## Steps

1. Open the Document Comfort popover from the annotation toolbar.
2. Enable Reader Mode.

<Figure alt="PLACEHOLDER for screenshots/reader-mode-toggle.png — before/after: normal toolbar view next to Reader Mode with chrome hidden — dark mode, 1600×1000, 2x" caption="Normal view compared to Reader Mode." />

## What you should see

The canvas expands to fill the window, editing controls hide, and the page becomes the focus.
