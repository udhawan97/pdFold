## Steps

1. Click **Reader Mode** in the toolbar, or press <kbd>⌘⇧R</kbd>.

<Figure src="/Orifold/assets/screenshots/reader-mode-toggle.png" alt="Reader Mode active: the sidebar and annotation toolbar are hidden, leaving just the page title bar, page content, and page navigation controls." caption="Reader Mode, with the sidebar and toolbar tucked away." />

## What you should see

The canvas expands to fill the window, editing controls hide, and the page becomes the focus. Press <kbd>⌘⇧R</kbd> again (or use the pill that appears) to exit.
