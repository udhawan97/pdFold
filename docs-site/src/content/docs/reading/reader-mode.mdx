## Steps

1. Open the Document Comfort popover from the annotation toolbar.
2. Enable Reader Mode.

<Figure src="/Orifold/assets/screenshots/reader-mode-toggle.svg" alt="Left: the normal workspace with sidebar and toolbar. Right: Reader Mode, with the chrome hidden and the page filling the window." caption="Normal view compared to Reader Mode." />

## What you should see

The canvas expands to fill the window, editing controls hide, and the page becomes the focus.
