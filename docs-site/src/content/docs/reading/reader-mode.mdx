<Callout variant="whentouse">
	You're reading or studying rather than editing, and want text editing and signing locked out of the way.
</Callout>

## Steps

1. Click **More** (⋯) in the toolbar, then **Reader Mode**, or press <kbd>⌘⇧R</kbd>.

<Figure src="/Orifold/assets/screenshots/reader-mode-toggle.png" alt="Reader Mode on: a Reader pill appears above the page next to a banner explaining that text editing and signing are hidden while study tools stay available." caption="Reader Mode locks editing and signing; study tools like notes and comments stay one tap away." />

<Callout variant="note">
	Reader Mode doesn't hide the sidebar or toolbar — it locks out text editing and signing specifically, so you can't accidentally change a document you're just reading. A **Reader** pill appears above the page as a reminder, with quick access to comments and Document Comfort.
</Callout>

## What you should see

A **Reader** pill appears above the page. Text editing and signing tools are locked (they'll explain why if you try to use them); study tools — comments and Document Comfort — stay available from the pill. Press <kbd>⌘⇧R</kbd> again, or use the pill's close button, to exit.
