## Steps

1. Click **More** (⋯) in the toolbar, then **Document Comfort**.
2. Pick a reading preset under **Reading Presets**: **Default**, **Night**, **Eye Care**, or **Focus**.
3. Open **Fine-tune** for direct control over **Warm Tone**, **Page Brightness**, and **Text Contrast**, plus **Eye Care** toggles for **Reduce Glare**, **Soften White Pages**, and **Reduce Animations**.

<Figure src="/Orifold/assets/screenshots/night-mode-comparison.png" alt="The Document Comfort popover open, showing Reading Presets (Default, Night, Eye Care, Focus), Application and Page Mode options, and Fine-tune sliders for brightness, contrast, and warmth." caption="The Document Comfort popover." />

## What you should see

The page tint, brightness, and contrast change immediately; switch presets or fine-tune settings anytime from the same popover. Your choice persists across documents.
