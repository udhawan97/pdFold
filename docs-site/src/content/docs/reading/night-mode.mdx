## Steps

1. Open the Document Comfort popover from the annotation toolbar.
2. Enable Night Mode.
3. Choose a warmth preset: **Gentle** (a light warm tint), **Paper** (a cream, paper-like tone), or **Amber** (the warmest, dimmest option for late-night reading).

<Figure src="/Orifold/assets/screenshots/night-mode-comparison.svg" alt="The same document page shown three times under the Gentle, Paper, and Amber Night Mode presets — each progressively warmer and dimmer." caption="Gentle, Paper, and Amber warmth presets side by side." />

## What you should see

The page tint and brightness change immediately; switch presets anytime from the same popover.
