<Callout variant="whentouse">
	You're looking for a specific word or phrase somewhere in your workspace.
</Callout>

Press <Kbd>⌘F</Kbd> to open search. It searches across every document currently imported into the workspace, not just the page you're viewing.

<Callout variant="note">
	Scanned pages have no searchable text until you run OCR on them.
</Callout>
