## Find

Press <Kbd>⌘F</Kbd> to open search. It searches across every document currently imported into the workspace, not just the page you're viewing. Use <Kbd>⌘G</Kbd> and <Kbd>⌘⇧G</Kbd> to step through matches.

<Callout variant="note">
	Scanned pages have no searchable text until you run OCR on them.
</Callout>

## Replace

Reveal the replace options from the search bar to update matching text. Replace one match at a time, or **Replace All…** with a confirmation step (undoable in one step).

<Callout variant="warning">
	**Replace edits your own comments only.** Matches inside the PDF's page text are find-only — Orifold won't rewrite the source document's text through search. To change text on the page itself, use [Edit existing PDF text](/Orifold/edit/edit-text/).
</Callout>
