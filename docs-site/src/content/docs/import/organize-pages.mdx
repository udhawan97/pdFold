## Steps

1. Open the sidebar page list.
2. **Reorder** — drag a page to a new position, even into a different document's section.
3. **Rotate** — right-click a page and choose a rotation.
4. **Delete** — right-click a page and choose delete, or select it and press Delete.

<Media poster="/Orifold/assets/gifs/reorder-rotate-delete-pages.svg" alt="A document's pages expanded in the sidebar with one page selected and rotated, and a right-click menu offering Rotate, Duplicate, and Delete page." caption="Rotating and deleting a page from the sidebar." spec="4–6s · right-click a sidebar page → Rotate, then Delete" />

<Callout variant="tip">
	⌘Z is fearless. Page deletes, rotations, and edits all live in undo history — experiment freely.
</Callout>

## What you should see

The sidebar reflects your new order immediately; the canvas jumps to whichever page you last touched.
