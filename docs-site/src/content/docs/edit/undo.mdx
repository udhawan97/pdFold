<Callout variant="whentouse">
	You deleted, rotated, or edited something and want it back.
</Callout>

Press <Kbd>⌘Z</Kbd> to undo and <Kbd>⌘Y</Kbd> to redo. This covers page deletes, rotations, text edits, and annotations.

<Callout variant="tip">
	Undo is fearless here — experiment with reordering and deleting pages, knowing you can always step back.
</Callout>
