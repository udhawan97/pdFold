	You imported a scan or a photo of a document and ⌘F finds nothing on the page.
</Callout>

## Steps

1. Open the scanned page in your workspace.
2. Run OCR from the page or document menu.
3. Wait for recognition to finish — it runs entirely on your Mac.

<Callout variant="tip">
	OCR runs locally using Apple's Vision framework. A scan of your passport, a lease, or a medical form never leaves the machine.
</Callout>

## What you should see

⌘F now finds text on the page, and the recognized text can be edited the same way as any other detected text.
