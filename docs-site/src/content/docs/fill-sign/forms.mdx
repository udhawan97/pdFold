## Steps

1. Open the PDF; Orifold detects its form fields automatically.
2. Click a field and type your answer, or check a checkbox.
3. Use the inspector to jump between fields and confirm nothing's missed.

## What you should see

Every detected field is fillable directly on the page, with your answers visible as you type.
