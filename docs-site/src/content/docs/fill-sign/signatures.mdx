## Steps

1. Select the signature tool from the annotation toolbar.
2. Draw your signature with your cursor or trackpad.
3. Place it on the page and resize it as needed.
4. Export the signed PDF.

<Figure alt="PLACEHOLDER for gifs/sign-document-workflow.gif — draw a signature, place it on a demo signature line, then export — dark mode, 1600×1000, 2x" caption="Drawing and placing a signature." />

<Callout variant="tip">
	No e-sign service, no upload — your signature is drawn and placed entirely on your Mac and never touches a server.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="note">
	A drawn signature in Orifold is a visual mark, not a cryptographic digital signature. For documents that require a verifiable digital signature (some legal and government filings), check what your recipient actually requires.
</Callout>

## What you should see

The signature appears on the page exactly where you placed it, and travels with the PDF once exported.
