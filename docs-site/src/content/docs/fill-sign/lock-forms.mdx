## Reset a form

Choose Reset Form to clear every field back to blank.

## Lock answers at export

When exporting, choose to lock form answers. The exported PDF keeps its filled values but recipients can no longer change them.

<Callout variant="tip">
	Flatten before sharing: locking form answers and baking in any stamps at export means nobody can quietly edit your final copy.
</Callout>

## What you should see

Locked exports open normally in any PDF viewer, showing your answers as plain, non-editable content.
