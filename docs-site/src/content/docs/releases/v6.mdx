*Released July 3, 2026*

Orifold v6 expands the app from document assembly and markup into a fuller PDF finishing workflow.

- **Searchable scans with local OCR** — Vision OCR recognizes scan text and writes it back as searchable text, validated before it becomes part of the workspace. See [OCR scanned pages](/Orifold/edit/ocr/).
- **PDF forms** — field detection, reset, and locking answers by flattening at export. See [Fill PDF forms](/Orifold/fill-sign/forms/).
- **Stamps, watermarks, page numbers, and Bates labels** — burned into exported PDFs, following page order. See [Stamps, watermarks & Bates labels](/Orifold/annotate/stamps/).
- **Protected PDF export** — passwords and permissions verified before and after encryption. See [Password-protect a PDF](/Orifold/export/protect/).
- **File-size reduction** — image downsampling for oversized PDFs. See [Compress a PDF](/Orifold/export/compress/).
