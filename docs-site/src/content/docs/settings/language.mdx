<Callout variant="whentouse">
	Orifold launched in the wrong language, or you'd rather use it in one of the other five supported languages.
</Callout>

## Steps

1. On the landing screen, open the language switcher.
2. Choose English, Español, Français, हिन्दी, 简体中文, or 日本語.

<Figure src="/Orifold/assets/screenshots/language-switcher.svg" alt="The landing-screen language switcher open, listing English, Español, Français, हिन्दी, 简体中文 and 日本語." caption="The language switcher on the landing screen." />

## What you should see

The interface updates immediately in the language you chose, and your choice is remembered across future launches.

<Callout variant="note">
	Each language name appears in itself in the switcher, so even if you're lost in the wrong language, you can still find yours.
</Callout>
