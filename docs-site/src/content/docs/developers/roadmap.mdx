Orifold is in beta on the road to 1.0. Direction over dates — the list below is intent, not a schedule. The paper isn't done folding.

<Callout variant="tip" label="A friendly sneak peek, not a blood oath">
	Orifold is built part-time, so the order may shuffle and a fold or two may take the scenic route. The direction holds steady, though: more useful, more polished, more delightful — one crease at a time. Softly stated on purpose — “on the radar,” never “shipping Tuesday.”
</Callout>

## 🛠️ Now — on the bench

Steadying what already ships — signing, the qpdf engine, the companions, localization, and the documentation you're reading. Chief among them: **sturdier editing** — text edits that are harder to surprise on rotated pages, in deep undo history, or when a file is quietly touched in another app between saves.

<Callout variant="tip" label="Now in beta: object editing">
	**Move the furniture** is live as of v0.8.8. Pick the **Select** tool, click a real graphic on the page — an image, a logo, a line, a shape — then move, resize, layer, or delete it. Edits go into the file's actual content (no ghosts, no leftover copies), compose with inline text edits, and survive save, reopen, and export with full undo/redo. Still growing: duplicate, recolor, multi-select, and rotated-page support. See [Move & edit objects](/Orifold/edit/edit-objects/).
</Callout>

## 🌫️ Later — quietly brewing

- **Automated UI smoke tests** to complement the unit suite.
- Broader OCR language coverage and export polish, as real use surfaces needs.

## 🚫 Non-goals

Being explicit about scope is part of the trust. Orifold is **not** planning to add:

- **Cloud sync or accounts.** The local-only boundary is the product, not a limitation to fix.
- **Telemetry or analytics.** There's no server to send it to, and there won't be.
- **Real-time collaborative review or co-editing.** That requires a cloud Orifold won't build.
- **Windows or iPad versions.** It's a native macOS app; a rewrite for other platforms isn't planned.

<Callout variant="note">
	A non-goal isn't a "no forever" so much as a "not without changing what Orifold is." If a feature would require a server or an account, it's out of scope by design.
</Callout>
