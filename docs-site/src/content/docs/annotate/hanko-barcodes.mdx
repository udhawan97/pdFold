## Place a hanko seal

1. Open the stamp palette.
2. Enter the name or short text for the seal.
3. Choose a circle or square preview, then click **Place hanko**.
4. Click the page where the seal should land. You can move or resize it before export.

<Callout variant="warning">
	A hanko in Orifold is a visual page decoration. It is not a certificate-backed digital signature and does not prove identity or document integrity.
</Callout>

## Generate and place a code

1. Choose **More → Insert barcode / QR**.
2. Select **QR**, **Code 128**, **Aztec**, or **PDF417**, then enter the payload.
3. Check the preview, click **Place**, and click the destination on the page.

## Scan the current page

Choose **More → Scan barcodes on this page**. Orifold uses macOS Vision on-device, lists detected payloads, and lets you copy a value or open a supported link. Nothing is uploaded.
