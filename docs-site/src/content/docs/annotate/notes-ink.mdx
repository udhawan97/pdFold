## Steps

- **Notes** — select the note tool, click where you want the note, and type your comment. It appears as a small icon on the page.
- **Ink** — select the ink tool and draw directly on the page with your cursor or trackpad.

## What you should see

Notes appear as clickable icons that expand to show their text; ink strokes render directly on the page. Both show up in the inspector's annotation list.
