## v0.8.1 — Hardened, verified, and honestly numbered

*Released July 2026*

<Callout variant="note" label="Beta">
	Orifold is in beta. `v0.8.1` marks the move to semantic versioning — the v0.1–v0.7 line
	continues here as focused polish, validation, and user-flow hardening on the road to 1.0.
</Callout>

v0.8.1 has no single headline feature. Instead it hardens everything v0.7 shipped — signing, the qpdf engine, the companions, localization — under real use, and gives the documentation the overhaul it was overdue for.

- **Signing, made trustworthy** — async signing with a working cancel button, a TSA fallback chain with a provider picker, certificate trust/revocation checks, persistent certificate profiles, and a post-export structural self-check. See [Sign a document](/Orifold/fill-sign/signatures/).
- **Toolbar and sidebar redesign** — a consolidated, responsive toolbar with Find & Replace and a dedicated Settings window; a redesigned sidebar with better undo grouping and performance. See [The Orifold window](/Orifold/get-started/the-window/).
- **Keyboard shortcuts overhauled** to match macOS conventions, with a new cheat sheet. See [Keyboard shortcuts](/Orifold/settings/shortcuts/).
- **Document Comfort replaces Night Mode** — four reading presets (Default, Night, Eye Care, Focus) plus warmth, brightness, and contrast controls. See [Document Comfort](/Orifold/reading/night-mode/).
- **Folder import** added to the intro screen and Add Files menu. See [Import files](/Orifold/import/import-files/).
- **Companions are bigger and more detailed** — both pets dock and hover larger so their folded-paper texture actually reads, and the first-launch companion shows off at full size for a few seconds before settling into its corner (hover it anytime to see that detail again; Reduce Motion skips the showcase). See [Meet Gami & Ori](/Orifold/get-started/companion/).
- **Two rounds of locale-staleness fixes** across companions, toolbar messages, and popovers, plus a fixed language-selector z-order bug. See [Change the app language](/Orifold/settings/language/).
- **A full documentation site rebuild** with in-app links from the Help menu — the pages you're reading now.

Full changelog: [`docs/release-v0.8.1.md`](https://github.com/udhawan97/Orifold/blob/main/docs/release-v0.8.1.md).
